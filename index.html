Campus Life

School church

Was established in 1963

The history of Holy Child Parish reflects the history of East Regina's growth and development. Originally, the Catholics of the area attended Little Flower Parish, but by the early 1960's, the people of the growing Glen Elm subdivision worked together to establish St. Joseph the Worker Parish, with their new church officially blessed on September 2, 1963.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The St. Theresa School Library is a modern, quiet space to read and study combining classroom teachings and inspire lifelong learning. Preschool visits each week for a read-aloud story time and can check out age appropriate books.

School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

An auditorium is a room built to enable an audience to hear and watch performances. Our school has an Auditorium which is instrumental in the smooth functioning of various activities organized by the school.
